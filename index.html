About

Coco's Chocolatiers was founded in 2019, by world renowned chocolatier from Ottawa, Coco Cosma. His mission was to create delisous, fairtrade certified and organic certified choclates. His unique recicpes have been passed down generation to generation and he is now ready to share his recipes with the world. Both himself and his famiy have since 2018 opended four shops in Ottawa alone, each owned by one of his family members. Recently, due to the sudden success of Coco's Chocolatiers, Coco Cosmo has been interviewed on The Today Show, Jimmy Kimmel, and Goodmorning America.
